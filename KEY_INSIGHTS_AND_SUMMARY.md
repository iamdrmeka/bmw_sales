# BMW USED CARS SALES PERFORMANCE FOR YEARS 2010 TO 2024

# Summary and Key Insights

## Financial Performance

    - Total revenue for the period under review stood at approximately $19 trillion.

    - Total Units sold under the period stood at 233.38 million vehicles.

    - Revenue increased by 4.06% between 2010 and 2024 indicating slow long term growth.

    - 2022 was the most profitable year, while 2023 recorded the lowest profitability.

    -Growth trends shows a cycle of negative and positives, suggesting market response to external, regional or global factors.

## Regional Performance

    - Asia came out as the most profitable region, generting the highest revenue contribution, highest sales contribution and highest vehicle prices.

    - Africa ranked the lowest, while appearing 5th in total sales, majorly due to having the lowest average vehicle pricing.

    - The Middles East recorded the highest revenue growth (12.64%), representing the most promising expansion.

    - South America was the only region with negative growth(-5.28%) indicating potention market rejection.

    - Sales volume accross regions remain relatively balanced, averaging between 41-42 million units per region.

## Model perfomance

    - The 7 series was the top_performing model globally in both sales and revenue.

    -The BMw M3 recorded the lowest global sales and revenue

    - pricing was not really considered a factor in model sales performance as higher_priced vehicles maintained strong sales, suggesting brand perception and vehicel specifications affected customer's choices

    - While the 7 series led globally in total sales:
        - The X6 led sales in South America
        - The i8 led sales in Europe
        - The X1 led in Asia
        - The 5 series led in Africa

        - The 7 series dominated in the Middle East and North Americare

## Fuel Type

    - Hybrid vehicles were the top sellers globally.

    - Asia and North America showed a good adaptation to eco_friendly vehicles.

    - The middle East and Africa remained petrol_dominated

    - South America is the only Diesel dominated market.

    - Diesel recorded the lowest overal global sales.

## Engine size & Mileage

    - The 30.L -3.9l engine varaints generated the highest sales globally and regionally.

    - 5.0L variants were the least prefereed accross all regions.

    - Lower mileage vehicles carried a higher price tag than the higher mileage vehicles, however the higher_mileage vehicles recorded  stronger sales volume, indicating that customers are going for more affordable option.

## ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS

    - Highest revenue and sales generation comes from the Asian market.

    - The middle East has  great potential for expansion and growth.


    - Effort should be put in place to produce more hybrid and eco friendly options as there is a global shift to eco-friendly cars.

    - All regions must maintain peculiar pricing options to ensure fair competion in the local market.
